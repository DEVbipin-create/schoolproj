About Us

With an objective of generating quality education to our pupils via innovative teaching techniques, child-friendly environment, and through fully digitalized classrooms, which is the only necessity of the ever-changing world, Oxford Secondary School was established in the year 1991 A.D. to facilitate the public of Lumbini province.
